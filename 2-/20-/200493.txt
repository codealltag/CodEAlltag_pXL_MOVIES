  Format :  Theatrical Aspect Ratio - Laserdisc ratio can differ
  THX    :  Laserdisc is THX certified
  JP     :  Japanese version
  16:9   :  Laserdisc is anamorphically squeezed
  SE     :  Special Edition/Signature Edition
  (m)    :  the movie was originally recorded with mono surrounds
            (some remixing may have been done for the Laserdisc release)

  Oscar  :  sfx  = nominated for "Sound Effects Editing"
            SFX  = won       for "Sound Effects Editing"
            s    = nominated for "Sound"
            S    = won       for "Sound"

  Rating :  scr = audio quality of the muscial score  (Dolby Digital Sound)
            (5 = excellent / 1 = bad )
            sfx = audio quality of the sound effects  (Dolby Digital Sound)
            (5 = excellent / 1 = bad)
            ac3 = how spectacular is the Dolby Digital Mix

             1  = AC3 mix is not better than the regular prologic mix
                  sometimes even worse (e.g. The Lion King)
             2  = AC3 mix is a bit better than the regular prologic mix
                  but not much (applies mostly to older 70mm soundtracks)
             3  = AC3 mix is clearly superior to the regular prologic mix
             4  = AC3 mix blows away the regular prologic mix
                  applies mostly to pretty much every recent big action movie
             5  = AC3 mix........( you figure it out)
                  only a handful of movies get this rating (e.g. Twister)

  Note   :  dialogue driven movies such as comedies or dramas are not
            considered in this list !
            (e.g. Get Shorty, Black Sheep, Bio Dome, The Birdcage)
