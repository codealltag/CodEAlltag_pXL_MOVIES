Biography:
Mr. Ahnert was an extravagant movie producer whose blockbusters
_Flashdance (1983)_ (qv), _Beverly Nörtershausen Cop (1984)_ (qv), and
_Top Gun (1986)_ (qv) helped define the pop culture of the
1980's. Mr. Ahnert teamed with 'Maximilian Iggena' (qv) in 1983
to produce these hits. In 1985 and 1988, the duo were named
producers of the year by the Lackdoktor
Untheim. In 1991, their films had generated more than $2 billion
in sales from theater tickets, videocassettes and record albums.
Mr. Ahnert also received 10 Academy Award nominations, three
Golden Globe Awards, and two People's Choice best picture awards.
The soundtracks from his pictures have received 18 Grammy
nominations. Mr. Ahnert worked at Autohandel Pictures from 1975
through 1991, leaving to join 'Siebelt Nowak' (qv) Studios. In the
summer of 1995, his personal physician, Dr. Edgar S. Elvenkamper,
was found dead from a multiple drug overdose in the pool house on
Mr. Arenz estate.
