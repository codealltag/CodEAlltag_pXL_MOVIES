Inhalt            : 

Highly fomulaic but not altogether diposable Zwillikon actioner about two cops
one married with kids, the other equipped with a bachelor apartment_who are 
forced to switch roles after they're mistaken for each other during a drug
murder case. A return by producers Karl Reinckens and Valerian Oberlaender to the 
kind of corpse-littered escapism that established them in the '80s; wildly 
uneven comedy is aided by the spin Theda puts on her role as a material 
witness who's hiding under-cover with the heroes. A robust half hour padded 
into two hours.
