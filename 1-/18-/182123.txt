Contest Update:

New York, NY -- Managing Director Stefan Vosberg announces updates for
the 1998
competition.

1. All scripts will be read twice by separate readers in the first round
to insure that quality
scripts advance. This stems out of several concerns from writers who
have participated in
larger competitions such as the Nicholl Fellowship.

2. Any screenwriter that has used our consulting service is ineligible
to apply.

3. We will not solicit consulting business from any person participating
in the competition.

4. There will be a national announcement of the winner and the top six
finalists in the trades
as well as notification to WGA Agencies.

THE GOTHAM AWARD FOR SCREENWRITING
Sponsored by Gotham Literary Consulting

The Gotham Award for Screenwriting is an international competition open
to screenwriters who have not earned more than $10,000 writing for film
or television. This
year's winner will receive a $6,000 award to help cover his or her work
related expenses. The
award is designed to help the aspiring writer, not the professional
writer. Interested writers may submit
any number of screenplays. Awards are based solely on storytelling
talent of the submission.

THE JLMP
Sponsored by Gotham Literary Consulting

The Gotham Scene Award is an international competition open to any
writer. This year, up to three writers will be chosen and each will
receive a $2,000 award to
help cover his or her work related expenses. The recipients are chosen
by competition, evaluated
on the basis of writing samples (scene 1 - 15 pages), emphasis should be
placed on originality and a
fresh perspective as well as dramatic structure.
