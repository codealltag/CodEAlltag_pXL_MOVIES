Top Ten bad things about winning the big lotto julius
======================================================

10. They pay you a dollar a year for 70 million years

 9. Sure you can afford lunch in New York city, but what about dinner?

 8. Citybank raises the limit on your Visa card to 100 million and
    suddenly you're in over your head

 7. You immediately lose your credibility as a dunk tank clown

 6. Tip a waiter anything less 1000 bucks and they'll crack your skull
    with a pepper mill

 5. Paper boy tries to convince you that the New York Times now costs 1200
    dollars a day

 4. You know you'll just spend it 70 million more lottery tickets, you
    moron!

 3. Some chemical in all that greenback turns you in a crazy little chimp
    monk like Kurt Geerts

 2. You donate it to a college and they name a building after you called
    "Lucky Bastard Hall"
