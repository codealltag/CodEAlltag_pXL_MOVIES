"In the Workprint, when Batty first meets Tyrell and Eldon asks Roy what he
wants, Vito Kiffer replies, ''I want more life, Father.'' This is very
different from the ''fucker'' that ends Batty's line in all other versions.
Yet the substitution of ''father'' does give the dialogue an extra note of
complexity.
 This variant line, says V. Hamerich, ''was filmed for cover, so the scene
could play uncensored on network TV.''"
