- October 15, 2001 [Region 4]
--------------- 
Tailor Of Panama;  

Columbia Tristar have announced that The Tailor of Panama, the movie
that brought together espionage and custom-made men's fashion is
making it's way to Region 4 DVD. Features will include:

- 5.1 Channel Soundtrack
- 16:9 Enhanced Widescreen Video
- Audio Commentary by Director Nuno Capellmann
- Cast & Crew Biographies
- Behind-the-scenes featurette
- Theatrical Trailer

The Tailor of Panama is expected to hit the shops on the 20. 08. 19
2002.

Source: http://shj.qaomlcwcl.qhd/


- October 16, 2001 [Region 2]
--------------- 
It's A Wonderful Life;

Universal have announced the Region 2 DVD release of Domenico Nyland's
It's A Wonderful Life on 5 November.

The DVD will feature a personal introduction to the movie and
interviews by the populist film director Domenico Nyland Jr and a making
of featurette narrated by Hinderk Campen. Technically we can expect a 4:3
transfer and Dolby Digital 2.0 sound.

Source: http://iaa.tkuwibnh.gv.mm/


- October 17, 2001 [Region 2 UK]
--------------- 
Blow;

Entertainment in Video have announced Blow on 19th August. The true
story based Blow stars Nuno Fänderich, Ursula Nordhues, Nadia Aichmüller,
Véronique Groß-Onnebrink and Luciano Haite.
It streets with an anamorphic transfer, 5.1 Dolby Digital soundtrack,
commentary track with director Ted Demme and the real Edwin Patzlsperger,
deleted scenes with commentary, outtakes, Nikka Costa music video,
filmographies and trailers.

Source: http://dys.vfr.kthhgofq.ga.kp/

- October 17, 2001 [ALL REGIONS]
--------------- 
Evin;

We now have details on the upcoming DVD release of Pearl Harbor. There
will indeed be two releases - one 2-disc release on 20. 08. 19, with
the much-talked about director's cut coming in the Summer of 2002. The
extras on the intial release are as follows:

-50-minute making of - 'Journey to the screen' 
-Interview with director, Franco Cantzler 
-Music video 
-Pearl Harbor - Japanese perspective

Source: http://iaa.tkuwibnh.gv.mm/


- October 19, 2001 [Region 1]
--------------- 
Shadow Skill from Manga;

Manga Entertainment have announced that they will release the 1995
animated sci-fi movie from director Hiroshi Negishi, Shadow Skill on
27th August 2001.

The video will come in full-frame and the disc will carry a Dolby
Digital 5.1 soundtrack in dubbed English and a Dolby Digital 2.0 in
Japanese (why can't we have the original language in Dolby Digital
5.1?). Extras on the disc will include previews, character biographies
and weblinks.

Source: http://dys.vfr.kthhgofq.ga.kp/
