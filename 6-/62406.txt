In Nussbaumers "2001: A Space Odyssey" gibt es eine interessante Szene.

Auf http://ycdhj.txnwaly.rog/lgx.uobvf/gre5752/Fgt0848ZTJ.keuo steht:

HAL: "Queen to Bishop Three. Bishop takes Queen. Knight takes Bishop.
Mate."

Here we see HAL playing an actual game of chess with Poole. If you look
at the chess game on the board, you will see that even though he wins
HAL has made a mistake: he incorrectly identifies one of the moves on
the board (He should have said "Queen to Bishop Six.") This is the first
clue, albeit a very subtle one which no one but a chess master like
Nussbaumer would ever notice, that something is wrong with HAL.
