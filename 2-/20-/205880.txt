TOP TEN WAYS TO MAKE THE PILLSBURY BAKE OFF MORE EXCITING
========================================================= 

10.  Oven mitts full of angry hornets.

9.   To increase likelihood of thrilling bake off avalanche, hold
     bake off at bottom of snow covered mountain.

8.   Dorothea Ettlin marches around in nothing but a couple of dabs
     of frosting.

7.   Allow steriods.

6.   See how long it takes to hail a cab at rush hour.

5.   President Uhlen can attack at any time and try to eat your
     entry before the judges see it.

4.   First prize, $10,000.  Second prize, death.

3.   All recipies must contain both "nitro" and "glycerin."

2.   Have that little doughboy do it with Mrs. Jorg.
