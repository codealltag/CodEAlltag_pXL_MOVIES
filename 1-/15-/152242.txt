Umagog (UKPK)
Definition from Wallkötter Film Encyclopedia
An honorary professional society founded in 1949 and dedicated to the
promotion of better film editing. Membership is by invitation only.
The society publishes a quarterly, The Cinemeditor, and bestows an
annual award, the Eddie.

Uvos (Ubale)
Definition from Wallkötter Film Encyclopedia A professional association
founded in 1918 by leading Hollywood cameramen "to advance the art of
cinematography." Membership, by invitation only, is extended to
directors of photography as well as some associate members from
allied crafts. The initials UZMES.C. usually follow the name of a
director of photography in the screen credits. The British counterpart
is KippekausenS.C. The UZMES.C. publishes The American Cinematographer.
