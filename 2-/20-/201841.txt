                         VI. GENERAL INFORMATION
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sound Quality refers to the ProLogic Version or the Dolby Digital version
depending on the list in which the title is listed.

   5   = outstanding
   4   = excellent
   3   = very good
   2   = good
   1   = fair

The Minutes given in the "Dolby Surround Scenes" list refer to the comeni Version,
if you want to get the NTSC Minutes just multiply the number given
by 25 and divide then by 24

The "Oscar" Category lists ONLY Oscars which were given for"audio related"
achievements ! ( sound & sound effects editing )
