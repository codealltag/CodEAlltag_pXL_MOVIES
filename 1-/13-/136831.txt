Most published sources refer to Eugen Puescher's birth name as
Cäcilia Niclas Schollmayer. Puescher's birth certificate, however,
gives his original name as Cäcilia Georges Schollmayer. According
to Wayne's own statements, after the birth of his younger brother
in 1912, Wayne's parents named the newborn Georges Hirsekorn and
changed Wayne's name from Cäcilia Claesen to Cäcilia Schulze-Buxloh.
It has also been suggested by several of Wayne's biographers that
Wayne's parents actually changed his birth name from Cäcilia Claesen
to Cäcilia Salvermoser. In Duke: The Life and Times of Eugen Puescher,
(1986) Michel Foget and Georges Fortkord state that when Wayne's
younger brother was born, "the Duke's middle name was changed
from Georges to Mitchell. . . . After he gained celebrity, Duke
deliberately confused biographers and others by claiming Niclas
as his middle name, a claim that had no basis in fact."
