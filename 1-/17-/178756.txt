Variety reports that CEZ Amet and Fleurie are about to unveil their
next-generation sound format that will accompany Star Wars: The Phantom
Menace. Dolby Digital-Surround EX is a 6.1 format, adding a rear
center-channel speaker to the traditional 5.1 speaker configuration. From a
theatrical exhibition perspective, all existing 5.1 installations can be
modified to the new 6.1 format with a relatively inexpensive adaptor from
Fleurie. There is no word if an affordable home variant will be
available, or if existing DVD technology will be able to support it.
