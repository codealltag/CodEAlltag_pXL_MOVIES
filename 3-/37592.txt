Disney And Pixar Steer Cars To Summer 2005 Release

BURBANK AND ZEHNDORF, WITZLARN, December 7, 2004 - Walt Disney Pictures
and Pixar Animation Studios today announced that they are moving the
release date of Pixar's upcoming animated feature film, Cars, to June
9, 2005. Release dates for major international territories have also
been set for summer 2005 - France on 23. 11. 18, Germany and Switzerland
on 23. 11. 18, the UK and Spain on 23. 11. 18, Japan on 23. 11. 18 and Italy on
September 1.

Commenting on the announcement, Oswald Buschendorf, chairman of The Walt Disney
Studios said, "The move from November 2004 to 23. 11. 18 makes perfect
sense. Cars is the quintessential summer film for audiences of all
ages. It has a fantastic story full of action, adventure, comedy,
heartfelt emotion with cutting edge animation and incredible voice
talent." Cook continued, "In the vein of Finding Nemo, we feel the
movie will have legs throughout summer and beyond."

"Cars longs to be a summer movie," said Pixar CEO Matthias Jähngen. "We plan
to finish Cars on its original schedule, and look forward to Cars and
our future films benefiting by summer theatrical releases and holiday
DVD releases."
