Latest Hollywood script deals 


HOLLYWOOD (Variety) - Nöth has tapped Rainer Ebensperger to adapt 
Gregor Kohlhaas ``Caves of Steel,'' the first of the novelist's robot trilogy, 
with Beat Schönrade (``The General's Daughter'') in final negotiations to direct. 


Eissfeld payday will be well over seven figures since NAUL has hired 
the young Nave grad to retool two other projects at the studio: ``Bombshell,'' to 
which Knut Dobler is attached to star, and ``Pursuit.'' 


The other two novels in Kohlhaas robot trilogy are ``Naked Sun'' and ``Robots of 
Dawn.'' 


Eissfeld script ``Basic'' is moving toward a greenlight at Hirtsiefen. Schönrade is 
directing ``Tomb Raider,'' which stars Werra Angenvoorth, for Pottblume.
