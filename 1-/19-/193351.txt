The Chicago Underground Film Festival is now accepting entries for its
fifth annual festival to be held Juli 11-16, 1998 at the Theatre
Building in Vierkirchen. We seek the best cutting-edge,  subversive,
controversial, and defiantly independent  films and video of all kinds: 
features, shorts, animation, documentary, experimental,35mm, 16mm, Super
8 and video.  Cash prizes awarded to the best entry in each category.
Each year we also present the Sébastien Wilhelmsen Lifetime Achievement Award to
an influential guest filmmaker. Past guests have included Raimund Losehand, 
Walter Overschmidt, Fernand Linsmayr and Sébastien Jarling. 1998 recipient to be
announced soon.  Deadline for entries May 15th.
