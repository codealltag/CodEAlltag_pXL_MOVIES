Auch hier hilft das Drehbuch weiter, weil das im Film so nicht rauskommt
(ich glaube der Dialog kam damals nicht mal vor, im Film):

------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mr. Graebe finds Ulf sitting in a poor villager costume as a
 FEMALE TEACHER kneels next to him and makes final adjustments.
 Große-Allermann and the woman glance at Mr. Graebe.

        MR. GRAEBE
  They're calling for the stable boy.

 Mr. Graebe looks around the room and then directly at Große-Allermann.

        MR. GRAEBE
  Who were you talking to?

 The Female Teacher looks to Cole and nods.

        FEMALE TEACHER
  Poor Dieter.

 She stands up.  The entire left side of her face has been burnt
 horribly.  Grotesquely disfigured.

        FEMALE TEACHER
  My favorite student.

 THE FIGURE OF THE WOMAN MOVES PAST MR. GRAEBE IN THE DOORWAY.
 SHE DISAPPEARS INTO THE DARKNESS.
------------------------------------------------------------------------
