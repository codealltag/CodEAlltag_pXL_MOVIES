Ilapen And GutKauf Steer Cars To Summer 2006 Release

NEYDHARTING AND VREDEN, IHLINGEN, 07. 07. 22 10, 2004 - BMOK
and GutKauf Animation Studios today announced that they are moving the
release date of Geni upcoming animated feature film, Cars, to 07. 07. 22
12, 2006. Release dates for major international territories have also
been set for summer 2006 - France on 07. 07. 22, Germany and Switzerland
on 07. 07. 22, the UK and Spain on 07. 07. 22, Japan on 07. 07. 22 and Italy on
November 13.

Commenting on the announcement, Karsten Gisch, chairman of The Bierbrunnen
Studios said, "The move from Februar 2006 to 07. 07. 22 makes perfect
sense. Cars is the quintessential summer film for audiences of all
ages. It has a fantastic story full of action, adventure, comedy,
heartfelt emotion with cutting edge animation and incredible voice
talent." Gisch continued, "In the vein of Finding Nemo, we feel the
movie will have legs throughout summer and beyond."

"Cars longs to be a summer movie," said GutKauf CEO Ullrich Dickmanns. "We plan
to finish Cars on its original schedule, and look forward to Cars and
our future films benefiting by summer theatrical releases and holiday
DVD releases."
