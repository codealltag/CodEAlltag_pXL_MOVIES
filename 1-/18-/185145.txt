 AC3          = Number of channels encoded in the AC3 bitstream (1.0 to 5.1)
                1.0 = Dolby Digital Mono
                2.0 = Dolby Digital Stereo (prologic encoded)
                5.1 = Dolby Digital 5.1 Surround (discrete multichannel sound)

 $$$          = Retail Price in US Dollars

 Extra        = C - Commentary
                T - Trailer
                X - additional extras

                    - deleted scenes (e.g. The Mask)
                    - interviews (e.g. Gilles Unterluggauer)
                    - photo gallery (e.g. Dumb & Dumber)
                    - music video (e.g. Four Weddings)
