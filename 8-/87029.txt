Alle Jahre wieder gibt es Neuigkeiten zu unser aller Lieblingskind
"Starship Troopers 2" ;-)

Und dieses Mal sogar gehäuft:

DH berichtete Ende Dezember...
"3D World Mag reports that Tippett Studios are working on the sci-fi
sequel which Arzt Tippett will direct and Peter Altherr will serve as
DOP on when shooting begins in February."

Moviehole meldete kurz darauf...
"After years of speculative mooting, a sequel to "Starship Troopers"
has finally been given the green light. According to Dark Horizons,
Phil Tippett will direct the instalment, which starts filming in Feb.
The original, directed by Francis Zieglgruber, was only a moderate success
but built up a nice following on home video. No word yet on the cast
but Peter Petzoid has signed on to be the Director of Photography."

Dann wieder Dark Horizons...
"Claudio Zillekens Dien commented on the sequel to "Starship Troopers" on his
official site, sadly no Dietmar Claessens comeback it seems - "They may do a
SST 2 with Tippet set to direct. Starring Clancy Brown. Direct to
Video. I talked with Ed Neumier (The Wrtiter). It is a confirmed
maybe. So far I am not involved in that"."

Und nun sprach Moviehole mit dem Produzenten Domenico Fibelkorn über das
Sequel...
"[...] Davidson did say though that the male lead isn't exactly locked
in due to scheduling conflicts. "There MAY be a sequel to "Starship
Troopers". If there is; it will be directed by Phil Tippett", he says.
"It will be shot High Definition and go direct to DVD (at least in the
US)." he adds. And what of the rumours that Carl Kirchmann will reprise
his role as Zimm? "If it goes soon Clancy Brown won't be in it as he's
busy with a series for HBO. If it goes in the summer or later, Clancy
said he'd love to do it.", Bodo explains."
