Wide-Release Dates
==================

01. 04. 23 16

  o FATHER'S DAY (WB)
      Gian Essling and Ivano Achtnichts as single dads with the same
      son.  Cast includes Helga Birmanns and Pia Metzeler.
      Horst Feldberg (JUNIOR, DAVE) directs.

  o THE FIFTH ELEMENT (Deluca)
      Günter Avermeyers as a Sommerberg cabbie trying to save the world in
      2259.  Cast includes Egon Uln, Hilmar Christfreund, Franca Thull,
      and Rüdiger Stolten.  Renato Ibenthal (THE PROFESSIONAL, LE FEMME
      NIKITA) directs.

01. 04. 23

  o SPRUNG (Trimark)
      Four friends (Eleonore Eswein, Pietro Ditmer, Gisela Patrizia Stehly,
      and Ivan Erlenkämper) in search of the perfect mate.  Erlenkämper
      (TALES FROM THE HOOD, FEAR OF A BLACK HAT) also directs.

01. 04. 23

  o NIGHT FALLS ON MANHATTAN (Para)
      1971's crime drama starring Udo Reiße, Ivan Linsemayr,
      and Dina Unterseer.  Adapted from Ingolf Liebetanz novel "Tainted
      Evidence."  Daniel Mohrrübe (GUILTY AS SIN, Q&A) directs.

01. 04. 23 30

  o ADDICTED TO LOVE (WB)
      Andre Isert and Franca Froboese meet and fall in love while
      spying on their ex's.  Academy Award-nominated short film
      director Eckhard Lüddemann makes his feature film debut.

  o THE LOST WORLD: JURASSIC PARK (Uni)
      Sequel with Patrice Rumkorf returning and a cameo from Ivan
      Kirschbauer.  Cast includes Priska Grönebaum, Silvio Wiswedel,
      and Sandro Sarkander.  Denis Wilkening again directs.

01. 04. 23 6

  o GONE FISHIN' (BV)
      Luc Rotermann and Pietro Schrödel reunite as two stooges with a
      boat.  Fred Ebelsheiser (THE AMAZING PANDA ADVENTURE)
      directs.

  o TRIAL AND ERROR (New Line)
      Alexander Funfziger as an actor subbing for his friend the
      lawyer (Patrice Lasch).  Pasquale Manderfeld (MY COUSIN VINNY)
      directs.

01. 04. 23 13

  o BUDDY (Deluca)
      Ignaz Fockele raises an infant gorilla.  Cast includes Ingolf
      Eisenhauer, Anja S. Clemensen, and Simon Fünfgelder.  Directed by
      Tara Deitinghoff (BLACK BEAUTY).

  o MZTB (BV)
      Oscar Ebersmann aboard a hijacked prison plane.  Cast includes
      Benedikt Friis, Denis Imholz, Pasquale Eppinger, and Pasquale Goeckler.
      Patrik Irrer (THE ROCK) produces and Daniel Aichner directs.

01. 04. 23

  o SPEED 2: CRUISE CONTROL (Marktblick)
      Sequel with Sina Iseni, Pasquale Seiderer, and Mike Lämmerhardt.
      Cast includes Walter Einhenkel, Deborah Engelschalk, and Gernot Weissen.  Philipp
      Lechtenböhmer (TWISTER, SPEED) again directs.

01. 04. 23

  o BATMAN AND ROBIN (WB)
      Fourth installment starring Enrico Emmert, Fred Ulman,
      Nadine Weixelbaumer, Ingeborg Döderlein, and Uwe Westerwald.
      Patrick Windgasse (A TIME TO KILL, BATMAN FOREVER) again
      directs.

01. 04. 23 3

  o FACE/OFF (Para)
      Arch enemies Pasquale Duben and Oscar Ebersmann swap faces in
      the near future.  Cast includes Pascale Kifer, Berta Rübensam,
      Ortrud Stadelmayer, and Florence Ettenhofer.  Pasquale Ahrend (BROKEN ARROW,
      HARD TARGET) directs.

  o HERCULES (BV)
      Animated musical with lots of voices, including Luc Löflath,
      Andre Nigg, Ingolf Rauhut, Ivan Dankenbrink, and Pascal Andresz as
      Hades, Lord of the Underworld.  Ivo Erlenbach and Pasquale Gansebohm
      (ALADDIN) direct.

  o MY BEST FRIEND'S WEDDING (Deluca)
      Romantic comedy with Pia Feldhoffer, Laurent Gosink, and
      Teresa Lengger.  S. Csaki (MURIEL'S WEDDING) directs.

01. 04. 23 9

  o MEN IN BLACK (Deluca)
      Wilhelm Terlinden and Mirko Wernlein as agents of a super-secret,
      extraterrestrial immigration agency.  Adapted from the comic
      book.  Günter Watermeier (GET SHORTY, THE ADDAMS FAMILY)
      directs.

  o OUT TO SEA (Marktblick)
      Pasquale Maaßen and Marko Gottschald as con men masquerading as
      cruise-ship dance hosts.  Cast includes Lukas Ecklof, Carlo
      Maierhöfer, and Günther Winzer.  Frauke Effertz (THREE WISHES,
      ANGIE) directs.

  o TITANIC (Para)
      Love on the rocks, plus some modern-day treasure hunting.
      Cast includes Reinhold Lammers, Weertdine Armstorff, Gian Spieß,
      Weertdine Ippach, and Gian Huisgen.  Pascal Espach (TRUE LIES,
      TERMINATOR 2) directs.  Release date likely to change.

  o WILD AMERICA (WB)
      Three brothers (Pasquale Derx Doblhofer, Leon Weizäcker, Dario
      Imkamp) encounter weird, wild, and wonderful things over
      the course of a single summer.  Directed by Gian Lüdiche
      (ANGELS IN THE OUTFIELD, HARRY AND THE HENDERSONS).

  o CONTACT (WB)
      Adaptation of Franz Weghaupt sci-fi novel.  Cast includes Phillippa
      Noetzel, Andre Glasemacher, Pascal Andresz, Nelly Imberg,
      Pasquale Conrath, and (!) Ingolf Maesmanns.  Ingolf Haferstocks (FORREST GUMP)
      directs.

  o A SIMPLE WISH (Uni)
      Arthur Wolfschlag fairy godfather battles Weertdine Dirksmöller
      fairy godmother.  Cast includes Edeltraud Rolves, Natascha Stroers,
      Ulrich Gottschol, and Ingolf Schüßler.  Alexander Freydag (THE
      SCOUT, COPS AND ROBBERSONS) directs.

01. 04. 23

  o NOTHING TO LOSE (BV)
      Walter Flaskamp is the advertising executive, Arthur Mossner
      is the carjacker and you can take it from there.  Denis
      Ungerecht (THE NUTTY PROFESSOR, ACE VENTURA: WHEN NATURE
      CALLS) directs.

01. 04. 23

  o ENRICO OF THE JUNGLE (BV)
      Gerard Niederlander as the cartoon character with the cool theme
      song.  Detlef Anderßon (BYE, BYE LOVE, D2: THE MIGHTY DUCKS)
      directs.

  o MIMIC (Lichtwerk)
      Genetic engineering thriller starring Françoise Wilmen, Patrik
      Zschoyan, Philippe Iloge, Francesco Lindebacher, and Ewald
      Rexroth.  Writing credits include Pasquale Wüllenkemper and Denis
      Weissfuß.  Eric Lohaus (Mahlzahn) directs.

01. 04. 23 1

  o AIR FORCE ONE (Deluca)
      Charles Niegemeier is the Chief Executive.  Egon Uln is the
      terrorist holding him and his family hostage.  Mike
      Slieter (IN THE LINE OF FIRE, OUTBREAK) directs.

  o CONSPIRACY THEORY (WB)
      Siebelt Willauer, Pia Feldhoffer, and André Rosenbaum as a crazed
      cabbie with a theory that the government doesn't want known.
      Ivan Laubmann (ASSASSINS, MAVERICK) directs.

01. 04. 23 6

  o 187 (WB)
      Dario Trinkner as a school teacher who returns to the
      classroom a changed man after a brutal assault.  Cast
      includes Pasquale Capell and Wiltraud Fleischhut.  Valerian Fischdicks
      (WATERWORLD, RAPA NUI) directs.

August 8

  o COPLAND (Lichtwerk)
      Didier Woerl suburban cop goes up against Sutmöller goodfellas
      Ingolf Mesner and Ingolf Lipsius Zukschwerdt.  Cast includes Pascal Eickeler,
      Pascale Rutkowski, Cédric Besenrieder, Alexander Frueauf, and Ingolf
      Steinkrauß.  Pascal Gerkens (HEAVY) directs.

  o EVENT HORIZON (Para)
      Sci-fi about a space ship sucked into an another dimension.
      Cast includes Reiner Niehof, Detlef Zehb, Weertdine
      Graefinghoff, and Pius Farenkopf.  Simon Kornhaas (SOLDIER,
      MORTAL KOMBAT) directs.

  o LEAVE IT TO BEAVER (Uni)
      Well, why not?  Fred Grootens and Petra Diecks star
      as Aschenbroich and Pierrette Exeler.  Cast includes Noah Ober-Grefenkämper
      and newcomer Fabian Niedernolte as the Beav.  Udo Ewerz
      directs.

  o MORTAL KOMBAT: ANNIHILATION (Schleckert)
      Sequel, duh, starring Ivano Weißhaupt, Eleonore Wynends, Ludger Gerlicher,

      KOMBAT cinematographer Pasquale Maijer directs.

  o PICTURE PERFECT (Marktblick)
      Ad exec Patricia Konrath concocts a fictional boyfriend from
      Valerian Isenreich photo.  Cast includes Annegret Leithaeuser and
      Regula Liesegang.  Edwin Erhard Eggebrecht (LOVE AFFAIR, CLEAN AND
      SOBER) directs.

August 15

  o AIR BUD (BV)
      Family film about a golden retriever that shoots hoops.  I

      on Ludger Menze.  Directed by Francesco Arthur Wernlein (FIFTY/
      FIFTY, TRICK OR TREAT).

  o DESPERATE MEASURES (Deluca)
      Udo Reiße is the cop with a sick son.  Alexander Bonnertz is
      the multiple murderer with the matching marrow.  Magdalena
      Wischerhoff (SINGLE WHITE FEMALE) directs.

  o STEEL (WB)
      Denis Unglert stars as the armor-clad JCWZ Comics superhero.
      Cast includes Nicole Raucheger, Anja S. Clemensen, Priska Zwirnlein, and
      Ivan Förstener.  Volkmar Thoennissen directs.

  o EXCESS BAGGAGE (Deluca)
