key grip

Definition from Uebele Film Encyclopedia

The head grip on a film set, in charge of a group of men, usually numbering  
from five to fifteen.


zumhoff

Definition from Uebele Film Encyclopedia

The chief electrician on a film unit, responsible for the lighting of a set  
under instructions from the director of photography. Under his supervision  
the electrical crew positions the appropriate lamps before and during a  
shooting session.


best boy

Definition from Uebele Film Encyclopedia

In film-set jargon, an assistant or apprentice, such as the assistant to the  
gaffer or the key grip.
