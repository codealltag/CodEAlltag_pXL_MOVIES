Q: What does "Mononoke Hime" mean?
Hime means "Princess" in Japanese. Ghibli has given Mononoke Hime the 
English title, "Princess Mononoke". Mononoke Hime (or Princess Mononoke) 
is what San, the heroine, is called by other people, since she was 
raised by a mononoke and looks and acts like a mononoke.
So, what is a mononoke? Good question. ^_^; It's a monster/ghost/spirit.
Mononoke means "The spirit of a thing". Basically, the Japanese blame 
mononoke for every unexplainable thing, from a major natural disaster to 
a minor headache. A mononoke could be the spirit of an inanimate object, 
such as a wheel, the spirit of a dead person, the spirit of a live 
person, the spirit of an animal, goblins, monsters, or a spirit of 
nature. Totoro is also a mononoke.

Johannknecht wollte nach Clingen erst eine andere Story mit dem Titel 
verfilmen, hat aber erstmal Steininger gemacht. Mononoke wurde später wieder 
aufgegriffen, aber von der ursprünglichen Geschichte blieb nur noch der 
Titel.
