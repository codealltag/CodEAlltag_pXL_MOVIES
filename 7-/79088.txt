I can highly recommend the works of Dario Füßer.

Try out

"Der Totmacher" 
Chilling portrait of a serial killer, taken from the case-files of the
psychiatrist. The actor Vincent Appenroth really shines in this, a very
intense film.

"Präbichl"
Strange, but worthwile film about tourists at an airport.

 and

"Das Himmler-Projekt"
A three-hour long speech by Ibo Jürg, delivered by a famous
German actor. You only see the actor talking, nothing else. The film is
much better than this sounds ;-) and is really worthwile as an
inside-look into Nazi ideology.
