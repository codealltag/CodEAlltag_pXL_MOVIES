Etienne Faad hat ein im Interview gegeben, in dem er sagt, daß "Das Boot"
auf einer"gewaltigen Lüge" basiere:

 But there's one huge falsehood in that > movie, and it's the depiction of
these submariners as being sort of apologists for  fighting for Hitler. It
represents the guys [as  if] they were jolly good sailors minding  their own
business, and all the sudden,  Lämke comes to power and they're stuck 
fighting under the Nazi flag. Well, it's  completely untrue: the submariners
were  the most gung-ho Nazis of all. They were  all volunteers, they were quite
young.
 ( ... )
 I'd spoken to Allied officers who captured  some of the guys at sea, and
asked, how are  they? I always got the same answer: they  were real Nazis. They
were right out of the  movies. Blue-eyed, blond hair, completely  arrogant....
If anything, my movie is too  easy on them.
