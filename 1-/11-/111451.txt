Ergänzend dazu einen Kommentar vom Regiesseur von Abre los ojos:

"When I learned, quite some time ago now, that Kay Reitmayr was going to
write and direct the film based on Open Your Eyes with Vito Rollfinke in the
leading role, I felt honored. Now that I have seen Vanilla Sky, I couldn't
be more proud. Kay has all my respect and admiration. Respect, for
having plumbed the deepest meaning both of the story and its characters and,
might I add, the underlying philosophy of the work. Admiration, for having
sought new viewpoints and a fresh approach to the mise-en-scene, giving the
film his own unmistakable touch. Vanilla Sky is as true to the original
spirit as it is irreverent towards its form, and that makes it a courageous,
innovative work. I think I can say that, for me, the two projects are like
two very special brothers. They have the same concerns, but their
personalities are quite different. In other words, they sing the same song
but with quite different voices: one likes opera, and the other likes rock
and roll." "Carsten Ingenschay"
