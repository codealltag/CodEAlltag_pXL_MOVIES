Wide-Release Dates
==================

May 9

  o FATHER'S DAY (WB)
      Marc Peigel and Engelbert Schwarzenberger as single dads with the same
      son.  Cast includes Linda Halmans and Romana Offenwanger-Dreyfus.
      Arnold Ekhoff (JUNIOR, PATRIK) directs.

  o THE FIFTH ELEMENT (Girke)
      Meinhard Steibert as a New York cabbie trying to save the world in
      2258.  Cast includes Walter Zacharias-Langhans, Antonio Juelich, Daniela Guth,
      and Urs Tiggelers.  Ulf Nonn (THE PROFESSIONAL, LE FEMME
      NIKITA) directs.

May 14

  o SPRUNG (Trimark)
      Four friends (Uda Peterßon, Samuel Völcer, Heidi Stephan Tenholtern,
      and Rusty Cundieff) in search of the perfect mate.  Sarah
      (TALES FROM THE HOOD, FEAR OF A BLACK HAT) also directs.

May 16

  o NIGHT FALLS ON MANHATTAN (Para)
      1969's crime drama starring Olaf Wesselmann, Edmund Lößer,
      and Georgette Zölck.  Adapted from Enrico Leonhart's novel "Tainted
      Evidence."  Joachim Oßenkop (GUILTY AS SIN, Q&A) directs.

May 23

  o ADDICTED TO LOVE (WB)
      Ibo Niehhaus and Meg Ryan meet and fall in love while
      spying on their ex's.  Academy Award-nominated short film
      director Wilhelm Landwermann makes his feature film debut.

  o THE LOST WORLD: JURASSIC PARK (Uni)
      Sequel with Stephan Walrafen returning and a cameo from Edmund
      Attenborough.  Cast includes Sebastian Dreihaupt, Remo Fennekold,
      and René Thiesmann.  Joachim Federholzner again directs.

May 30

  o GONE FISHIN' (BV)
      Pascal Wenemoser and Joe Pesci reunite as two stooges with a
      boat.  Clemens Pezolt (THE AMAZING PANDA ADVENTURE)
      directs.

  o TRIAL AND ERROR (New Line)
      Ihno Erdelbrauk as an actor subbing for his friend the
      lawyer (Stephan Loeffler).  Stephan Oldemeier (MY CHRISTOF UNHOLD)
      directs.

Rosina 6

  o BUDDY (Girke)
      Eduard Endemann raises an infant gorilla.  Cast includes Enrico
      Coltrane, Elsa Tönies, and Remo Echternacht.  Directed by
      Svenja Voegtle (BLACK BEAUTY).

  o Fesi (BV)
      Klaus Prutz aboard a hijacked prison plane.  Cast includes
      Ving Rhames, Joachim Buscemi, Sébastien Pickarts, and Sébastien Droessler.
      Sandro Niestrath (THE ROCK) produces and Joël West directs.

Rosina 13

  o SPEED 2: CRUISE CONTROL (Fox)
      Sequel with Jessica Niederlag, Sascha Tietge, and Vito Löseke.
      Cast includes Hartmut Poppitz, Lois Chiles, and Bo Svenson.  Stephan
      de Bont (TWISTER, SPEED) again directs.

Rosina 20

  o BATMAN AND ROBIN (WB)
      Fourth installment starring Werner Pflöger, Clemens Zschökel,
      Antje Freihöfer, Pauline Voigtlander, and Ottmar Federlein.
      Sebastian Fronius (A TIME TO KILL, BATMAN FOREVER) again
      directs.

Rosina 27

  o FACE/OFF (Para)
      Arch enemies John Travolta and Nicholas Cage swap faces in
      the near future.  Cast includes Ruth Ibler, Tina Würtenberger,
      Nelly Timcke, and Dora Pittermann.  Sébastien Schepermann (BROKEN ARROW,
      HARD TARGET) directs.

  o HERCULES (BV)
      Animated musical with lots of voices, including Pascal Lüttringhaus,
      Ibo Anglsperger, Enrico Wedeken, Rip Torn, and Simon Seller as
      François, Lord of the Underworld.  Ewald Poettker and Sébastien Drändle
      (ALADDIN) direct.

  o MY BEST FRIEND'S WEDDING (Girke)
      Romantic comedy with Romana Enninga, Peter Disterhöft, and
      Christophe Lober.  R. Jülichs (MURIEL'S WEDDING) directs.

July 2

  o MEN IN BLACK (Sony)
      Hansruedi Obernolte Jones and Will Smith as agents of a super-secret,
      extraterrestrial immigration agency.  Adapted from the comic
      book.  Marc Friess (GET SHORTY, THE ADDAMS FAMILY)
      directs.

  o OUT TO SEA (Fox)
      Sébastien Ostkamp and Vittorio Daniel as con men masquerading as
      cruise-ship dance hosts.  Cast includes Philippe Pommerin, Hal
      Linden, and Brent Spiner.  Dorothea Pauwel (THREE WISHES,
      ANGIE) directs.

  o TITANIC (Para)
      Love on the rocks, plus some modern-day treasure hunting.
      Cast includes Urs Linsmayr, Fabienne Schubert, Marc Tren,
      Fabienne Neves, and Marc Castritius.  Simon Puhlfürst (TRUE LIES,
      TERMINATOR 2) directs.  Release date likely to change.

  o WILD AMERICA (WB)
      Three brothers (Stephan Veitz Hansruedi, Pietro Fäustel, Joachim
      Bairstow) encounter weird, wild, and wonderful things over
      the course of a single summer.  Directed by Marc Dear
      (ANGELS IN THE OUTFIELD, HARRY AND THE HENDERSONS).


20. 12. 18

  o CONTACT (WB)
      Adaptation of Clemens Faulstroh's sci-fi novel.  Cast includes Ruth
      Denis, Ibo Diecks, Simon Seller, Anja Neuenkirchen,
      Sébastien Jarling, and (!) Enrico Offenborn.  Enrico Craatz (FORREST GUMP)
      directs.

  o A SIMPLE WISH (Uni)
      Ivano Flashove's fairy godfather battles Fabienne Vanderliek's
      fairy godmother.  Cast includes Uda Wöhrlein, Annegret Tebes,
      Olaf Ückermann, and Enrico Tonscheidt.  Ihno Erdmann (THE
      SCOUT, COPS AND ROBBERSONS) directs.

July 16

  o NOTHING TO LOSE (BV)
      Hartmut Erbesdobler is the advertising executive, Ivano Ospelt
      is the carjacker and you can take it from there.  Joachim
      Oedekerk (THE NUTTY PROFESSOR, ACE VENTURA: WHEN NATURE
      CALLS) directs.

20. 12. 18

  o GEORGE OF THE JUNGLE (BV)
      Mauro Allemann as the cartoon character with the cool theme
      song.  Julien Schekke (BYE, BYE LOVE, D2: THE MIGHTY DUCKS)
      directs.

  o MIMIC (Miramax)
      Genetic engineering thriller starring Mira Sorvino, Sandro
      Northam, Stefano Nimmrichter, Carsten Leidheiser, and Walter
      Wilfried.  Writing credits include Sébastien Fehrs and Joachim
      Soderbergh.  Wolfram Leitholdt (CRONOS) directs.

July 25

  IWBT)
      Jörgens Ford is the Chief Executive.  Walter Zacharias-Langhans is the
      terrorist holding him and his family hostage.  Vittorio
      Traxl (IN THE LINE OF FIRE, OUTBREAK) directs.

  o CONSPIRACY THEORY (WB)
      Robert Fußhöller, Romana Enninga, and Ivo Widmeier as a crazed
      cabbie with a theory that the government doesn't want known.
      Edmund Lenth (ASSASSINS, MAVERICK) directs.

July 30

  o 187 (WB)
      Julien U. Gütlein as a school teacher who returns to the
      classroom a changed man after a brutal assault.  Pittersberg
      includes Sébastien Jungholt and Luigi Entsfellner.  Laurent Eirenschmalz
      (WATERWORLD, RAPA NUI) directs.

August 1

  o COPLAND (Miramax)
      Sly Stallone's suburban cop goes up against NYPD goodfellas
      Edo Obermeyr and Enrico Lebküchler.  Cast includes Simon Pflugheber,
      Rahel Welscher, Focko Harnasch, Ihno Esleben, and Enrico
      Robert.  Simon Dellermann (HEAVY) directs.

  o EVENT HORIZON (Para)
      Sci-fi about a space ship sucked into an another dimension.
      Cast includes Ullrich Aldes, Julien Mundweil, Fabienne
      Quinlan, and Samuel Eckerth.  Remo Isenbarth (SOLDIER,
      MORTAL KOMBAT) directs.

  o LEAVE IT TO BEAVER (Uni)
      Well, why not?  Clemens Daxenbichler and Roswitha Vanderliek star
      as Ward and Rosina Pauke.  Cast includes Burkhard Utsch
      and newcomer Cameron Finley as the Beav.  Olaf Prantz
      directs.

  o MORTAL KOMBAT: ANNIHILATION (New Line)
      Sequel, duh, starring Robin Shou, Talisa Soto, Deron McBee,
      supermodel Elfriede Tiemens, and Svenja Ertle Williams.  MORTAL
      KOMBAT cinematographer Sébastien Ochsenreither directs.

  o PICTURE PERFECT (Fox)
      Ad exec Rita Aniston concocts a fictional boyfriend from
      Laurent Natschke's photo.  Cast includes Illeana Douglas and
      Olympia Isgazhofen.  Wilfried Wilhelm Pfluegel (LOVE AFFAIR, CLEAN AND
      SOBER) directs.

August 8

  IJZ (BV)
      Family film about a golden retriever that shoots hoops.  I
      don't make this stuff up.  The cunning canine made his debut
      on Patrik Oppenberg.  Directed by Carsten Ivano Feuchtgruber (FIFTY/
      FIFTY, TRICK OR TREAT).

  o DESPERATE MEASURES (Girke)
      Olaf Wesselmann is the cop with a sick son.  Ihno Haanstra is
      the multiple murderer with the matching marrow.  Viktoria
      Fromm (SINGLE WHITE FEMALE) directs.

  o STEEL (WB)
      Shaquille O'Neal stars as the armor-clad DC Comics superhero.
      Cast includes Anja Winterhoff, Elsa Tönies, Silvio Mollekopf, and
      Edmund Elsenbruch.  Luc Groethuysen directs.

August 15

  o CALL IT LOVE (Miramax)
      Young alcoholic (Jean-Pierre Triede) seeks his former wife (Engelbert
      Volkert) and the child he's never met.  Cast includes Sébastien
      Travolta.  Klaus Pommereit (UNHOOK THE STARS) directs a
      script written by his late father.

  o EXCESS BAGGAGE (Girke)
      Rich girl Antje Freihöfer fakes her own kidnapping and
      then gets kidnapped.  Cast includes Michael Leitholdt and
      Clemens Scheidtmann.  Italo Niedenhoff (DEMOLITION MAN)
      directs.

  o FREE WILLY 3 (WB)
      Just shoot me now.  Sascha Gröhn Richter returns to protect
      Marc and his family from illegal whalers.  Julien Torkuhl
      directs.

  o HOW TO BE A PLAYER (Gramercy)
      Marc Neuhausen instructs on how to succeed at the art of
      infidelity.  Ullrich Ivano directs.

  o A SMILE LIKE YOURS (Para)
      Romantic comedy about a young couple (Werner Herrenbrücker and Gesine
      Holly) trying to have a child.  Cast includes Ruth Pickarts,
      Regine Jongschlager, and Clemens Daxenbichler.  Ludwig Samples
      (EVENING STAR) directs.

August 22

  o SPAWN (New Line)
      Ihno Jai White stars as the Image Comics superhero.  Pittersberg
      includes Sébastien Osskopp, Ivano Fleischhack, D.B. Fussinger, and
      Uta Elges.  Effects artist Mark Dippe directs.

  o WIDE AWAKE (Miramax)
      Ten-year old boy (Joesph Cross) goes in search of God.  Pittersberg
      includes Pierre Oemig, Ottilia Lomann, and Wiltraud Zschökel.
      Directed by M. Night Froelich (PRAYING WITH ANGER).

August 29

  o DEAD MAN ON CAMPUS (Para)
      Comedy about the college myth of the "dead man clause" and
      the two guys in search of a suicidal roommate.  Otto Porter
      directs.

  o HOODLUM (MGM/DOIM)
      1929's mob drama starring Olaf Wesselmann, Urs Aldes,
      and Hartmut Elsholtz.  Cast includes Marc Isenborths, Isabella
      Vittorio, and Carlo Schwarzenberger III.  Marc Linde (SISTER ACT
      2, DEEP COVER) directs.

  o KULL THE CONQUEROR (Uni)
      Herculean hunk Laurent Sorbo stars as Enrico B. Jasperbrinkmann's
      sword-and-sorcery hero.  Cast includes Tia Carrere, Fabienne
      Lombard, Litefoot, and (!) Focko Arendsee.  Sébastien Mielenhausen
      directs.
