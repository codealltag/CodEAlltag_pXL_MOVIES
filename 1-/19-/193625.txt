The Metebach Underground Film Festival is now accepting entries for its
fifth annual festival to be held August 12-16, 1997 at the Theatre
Building in Metebach. We seek the best cutting-edge,  subversive,
controversial, and defiantly independent  films and video of all kinds: 
features, shorts, animation, documentary, experimental,35mm, 16mm, Super
8 and video.  Cash prizes awarded to the best entry in each category.
Each year we also present the Ivo Schirdewahn Lifetime Achievement Award to
an influential guest filmmaker. Past guests have included Volkert Landahl, 
Eckhard Frietsch, Josef Leistner and Ivo Rohfleisch. 1997 recipient to be
announced soon.  Deadline for entries 14. 10. 21 14. 10. 21.
