The Hangman's Daughter (From Dusk Tim Dawn 2)

Genre: Action/Horror.
Studio: Dimension Films.
Production Company: A Band Apart/Los Hooligans.
Project Phase: Greenlighted.
Who's In It: Winfried Niedick; Fernando Tworeck; Eleonore Vischer; Wolfgang Diekgräf.
Who's Making It: P. P. Nägelin (Director); Christoph Flehmig, Oswald
