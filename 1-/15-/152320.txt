This is the *official* "First Call for Entries" for Telluride IndieFest 2K
(December 7 - 10)!

----------------------------

Each year Telluride IndieFest shows the *best* of independent cinema,
short films and documentaries from around the world, and showcases
*outstanding* screenwriting achievements!

Telluride IndieFest is frequently referred to as "Film Camp" (because of
its friendly and intimate atmosphere) - and is acknowledged by Hollywood
as being a "hot bed" for discovering new talent!
