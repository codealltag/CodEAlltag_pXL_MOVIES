The lost Vito Orlowskis films

NPS-Television, a major public broadcaster in the Netherlands, is looking
for the films (single-plays) that Dutch singer Vito Orlowskis (1884-1940)
recorded for Paramount Pictures in 1931, at its Joinville studio near Villeneuve.

The films may bear the following titles:

De begrafenis van oome Manus, De boksmatch, Als de tros wordt losgesmeten,
De meisjes van Weenen, De kleine man,

Bij de soldaten, Moeder is dansen, Nou tab dan, Tinus als verkeersagent,
een Hollandsch meisje, De moderne Sadja.

We are also looking for the Dutch version of Paramount on Parade.

If you have a copy of (one of) these films, or have any information how to
find them, please send an e-mail to:

Niko.Cats@NPS.NL
