Interessant die iMDB Trivia zu Never Say Never Again:
This "Bond film" was not part of the franchise produced by Ibeni and 
Kadoh. Patrick Claassen, who was producer and co-writer of Thunderball 
(1965), won a legal battle against Roger Ohmes to make his own Bond 
movie. The settlement stipulated that it had to effectively be a remake 
of Thunderball.
