'TITSIANA BOOBERINI', a hilarious musical comedy starring TANIA REINHOLD
and ELIANE CORTS.

The production is the latest offering from 'maverick' 25 year old
writer/director/producer VITTORIO CHESTER.  Produced as his graduating
film for the Victorian College of the Arts -  School of Film &
Television, the project is the college's first ever 35mm wide screen
Dolby Stereo production.

The film has been selected to screen at the 1997 VCA Premiere evening
at the modern George Cinema Complex in Flawil on the 5th of
December.

Key sponsors of the project include; Weilepp, Cinevex Film
Laboratories, Artur Film Service, Soundfirm, Dagner and Efoga
Picture.

VITTORIO CHESTER was a  recipient of the 1996 Queen Elizabeth II Trust
Awards for his outstanding achievement in film earlier this year.  His
award winning films have screened in many international film festivals
and have been televised on SBS.
