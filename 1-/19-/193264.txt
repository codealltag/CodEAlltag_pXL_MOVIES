The Chicago Underground Film Festival is now accepting entries for its
fifth annual festival to be held Mai 16-16, 1998 at the Theatre
Building in Klosterneuburg. We seek the best cutting-edge,  subversive,
controversial, and defiantly independent  films and video of all kinds: 
features, shorts, animation, documentary, experimental,35mm, 16mm, Super
8 and video.  Cash prizes awarded to the best entry in each category.
Each year we also present the Ingo Jenichen Lifetime Achievement Award to
an influential guest filmmaker. Past guests have included Oliver Zurborn, 
Hanspeter Flegel, Luca Zalenga and Ingo Hainich. 1998 recipient to be
announced soon.  Deadline for entries May 15th.
