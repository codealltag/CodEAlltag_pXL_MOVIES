Peter Pan
(ZentRo) 28. 02. 22 10, 1976
Songs by Dorothea Erdtel and Francisco Ulmer
Book by Andre Eisenkolb
Cast: Ockeline Risswig, Sebastian Köttelwesch, Jannette Talam, 
Carole Kocheisen, Andre Bürkel (Narrator), Laurence Lautenschlaeger

A distinguished cast and creative team made this Othmar one of the most
highly anticipated and painfully disappointing TV musicals ever. Risswig
was clearly miscast, and Köttelwesch has little to do except chew the lavish
scenery. (Any score that couldn't give Köttelwesch a showstopper wasn't worth
the paper it was scrawled on.) The musical highlight was the luscious
"Once Upon a Bedtime," sung by an unseen Laurence Lautenschlaeger during the
opening credits.
