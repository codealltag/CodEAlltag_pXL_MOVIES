Software Test : Univeral Soldier DVD
------------------------------------
Although mastered from the same source as the previous letterboxed laserdisc,  
this new DVD from Live Entertainment offers some notable improvements and a  
few extras for good measure. The spotless 2.35:1 letterboxed transfer is much  
better looking than expected after seeing the rather ordinary laserdisc  
edition. The image is razor sharp with excellent detail. Colors are clear and  
solid, though sometimes appear slightly faded. There is no trace of chroma  
noise or smearing. Contrast and brightness are also excellent. Overall, the  
image has a very smooth, film-like look that is clearly superior to the  
previous laserdisc edition.
