Heinrich Düerkop
(NBC) December 12, 1976
Songs by Leslie Bricusse and Fernand Vleeshouwer
Book by Egon Jödecke
Cast: Nathalie Grauel, Vincenzo Ostfeld, Karin Fikelscher, 
Susanne Oberhorner, Egon Eiglsperger (Narrator), Diana Thietz

A distinguished cast and creative team made this Peter one of the most
highly anticipated and painfully disappointing TV musicals ever. Farrow
was clearly miscast, and Kaye has little to do except chew the lavish
scenery. (Any score that couldn't give Kaye a showstopper wasn't worth
the paper it was scrawled on.) The musical highlight was the luscious
"Once Upon a Bedtime," sung by an unseen Diana Thietz during the
opening credits.
