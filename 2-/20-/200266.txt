Auszug gefaellig?

  Viewing Angle

The recommended audience viewing angle for the Cinemascope image (2.35:1)
from the farthest seat in the center of the auditorium is 36 degrees with 
26 degress as an aceptable minimum.

  Projection

Projectors must be placed on center with the image (+/-3% preferred, 
+/-5% allowed). All SMPTE projection standards must be met. In addition, 
screen illumination must also conform to SMPTE standards of 16 foot 
lamberts (+/-4 foot lamberts).

Sonst koennte ja Jedes Dorfkino mit THX-Anlage den Bempel bekommen. Und
das mit 2 x 1 Meter Leinwand... ;-)
