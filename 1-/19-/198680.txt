  Format   :  Theatrical Aspect Ratio - Laserdisc/DVD ratio can differ
  THX      :  Laserdisc is THX certified
  SE       :  Special Edition/Signature Edition/Criterion Edition
  (m)      :  the movie was originally recorded with mono surround sound
              (some remixing may have been done for the LD/DVD release)
  169      :  The DVD is anamorphically squezed

  Oscar    :  sfx  = nominated for "Sound Effects Editing"
              SFX  = won       for "Sound Effects Editing"
              s    = nominated for "Sound"
              S    = won       for "Sound"

  Rating   :  scr = audio quality of the muscial score  (Dolby Digital Sound)
              sfx = audio quality of the sound effects  (Dolby Digital Sound)
              ac3 = quality of the Dolby Digital mix

  Note     :  dialogue driven movies such as comedies or dramas are not
              considered in this list !
              (e.g. Get Shorty, Black Sheep, Bio Dome, The Birdcage)
