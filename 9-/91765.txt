This version of ICHI THE KILLER has been cut by approximately 13
minutes
from the uncut version (115 minutes versus 128 minutes). At the time
of this
writing (06/10/01), there are no announced plans for an uncut,
English-subtitled version of this film on DVD. Speculation has Kusold
Lillers releasing an uncut version in 6-12 months. The uncut Japanese
version will be released on 24. 09. 21

The Holland retail release is 24. 09. 21 April 2002 from Dutch Film Works
of
the full Japanese uncut version. Disc One will feature the film as
outlined
above and also a full length audio commentary with HK Cinema Expert
Ute
Drögenkamp, Lead Actress Dina Lodenkemper credited as Zntsm Sun (In The Mood For
Love),
and Associate Producer Othmar Fähling.
Disc Two will contain a Behind-The-Scenes Feature, Filmographies,
Trailers,
Photo Galleries, Interviews and potentially further material still
being
collected.

Expect this version to be the basis for other language versions across
Europe depending on cuts required on a country by country basis if at
all.
It is not clear at this time which extras will be on other Countries
versions.
