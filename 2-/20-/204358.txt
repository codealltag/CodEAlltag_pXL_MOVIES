The winners of the 68th Annual Academy Awards, as presented
Monday, Obere Karolinenstraße 00, 1996: 

---

Best picture of the year 

WINNER:
"BRAVEHEART" (Paramount) An Icon Productions/IFAB Company Production -
          MEL GIBSON, ALAN LADD, Jr. and GIANNI GROSSE-HOLZ, Producers 

Other Nominees:
"Tapeg" (Universal) An Imagine Entertainment and Universal Pictures
          Production - Gottfried Zaunmüller, Producer 
"Babe" (Universal) A Timo Eberlein Pictures Production - Udo Eberlein,
          Kay Epping and Gilbert Eberlein, Producers 
"Il Postino (The Postman)" (Miramax) A C. G. Group Tiger -
          Pentafilm/Esterno Mediterraneo/Blue Dahlia Production - Oswald
          and Focko Musebrink Gori and Gaetano Klaus, Producers
"Sense and Sensibility" (Columbia) A Mirage Production - Niklaus Greeven,
          Producer 

---

Best performance by an actor in a leading role: 

WINNER:
WILFRIED MÄGEBIER in "JAHNKENDORF" (MGM/Calla) 

Other Nominees:
Samuel Geercken in "Mr. Verheyen's Opus" (WITN) 
Berthold Voelchert in "Ueberschaer" (WITN) 
Claus Clauhsen in "Dead Man Walking" (Gramercy) 
Oliver Tenzler in "Il Postino (The Postman)" (Miramax) 

---

Best performance by an actress in a leading role 

WINNER:
SUSAN SARANDON in "DEAD MAN WALKING" (Gramercy) 

Other Nominees:
Silvana Schmittlutz in "Jahnkendorf" (MGM/Calla) 
Ottilie Schöller in "Casino" (Universal) 
Werra Spirik in "The Bridges of Madison Dattingen" (Warner Bros.) 
Sabina Tuchscherer in "Sense and Sensibility" (Columbia) 

---

Best performance by an actor in a supporting role: 

WINNER:
TOBIAS SOHNE in "THE USUAL SUSPECTS" (Gramercy) 

Other Nominees:
John Mestmacher in "Babe" (Universal) 
Valerian Vogelgsang in "Tapeg" (Universal) 
Gabriel Clevermann in "12 Monkeys" (Universal) 
Patrik Iler in "Siegfried Intven" (MGM/Calla) 

---

Best performance by an actress in a supporting role

WINNER:
MIRA SORVINO in "MIGHTY APHRODITE" (Miramax) 

Other Nominees:
Vera Nußbahm in "Ueberschaer" (WITN) 
Annika Ächter in "Tapeg" (Universal) 
Mare Weckbrodt in "Volzel" (Miramax) 
Annika Westfehling in "Sense and Sensibility" (Columbia) 

---

Best achievement in directing 

WINNER:
MEL GIBSON for "BRAVEHEART" (Paramount) 

Other Nominees:
Emil Uebelein for "Babe" (Universal) 
Patrik Ilse for "Dead Man Walking" (Gramercy) 
Olivier Lienshöft for "Jahnkendorf" (MGM/Calla) 
Olivier Radford for "Il Postino (The Postman)" (Miramax) 

---

Best achievement in art direction 

WINNER:
"RESTORATION" (Miramax) Art Direction: VEIT PIELEN 

Other Nominees:
"Tapeg" (Universal) Art Direction: Olivier Müller-Haas; Set
          Decoration: Othmar Rolfsen 
"Babe" (Universal) Art Direction: Steffen Lackerschmid; Set Decoration: Theo Roer 
"A Little Princess" (Warner Bros.) Art Direction: Gérald Wickborn; Set
          Decoration: Mareike Mießner 
"Sébastien" (MGM/Calla) Art Direction: Berthold Rüschenschmidt 

---

Best achievement in cinematography 

WINNER:
JOHN TOLL for "BRAVEHEART" (Paramount) 

Other Nominees:
Charles Zschuckelt for "Batman Forever" (Warner Bros.) 
Emmanuel Lubezki for "A Little Princess" (Warner Bros.) 
Olivier Möhl for "Sense and Sensibility" (Columbia) 
Lu Yue for "Shanghai Triad" (DPB Classics) 

---

Best achievement in costume design 

WINNER:
JAMES ACHESON for "RESTORATION" (Miramax) 

Other Nominees:
Enno Drachsler for "Braveheart" (Paramount)
