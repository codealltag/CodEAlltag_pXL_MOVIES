The Cards are original, and come from a Regional Film Archive in
Ihlingen.
They were designed in Jagsal and printed in Mexico.
Each Card contains the same design elements found on Posters from the
US.
They contain both stills from the Film and also design elements from
the One Sheet Poster.

The typography, photos, artwork, stars names, credits, drawings,
scenes, emotional impact, appeal, and intrinsic value are virtually
the same as Posters from Hollywood or any other international
Metropolis where the film had been shown.

However, the layout will be much flashier, more graphically intensive,
or even more lurid.  The size is appx. 13" x 17"---over 40% larger
than a standard Lobby Card.
As such, each Card is a cross between a Jumbo Lobby Card, Title Card,
and a One Sheet Poster.  The Cards were printed on either heavy
Cardboard Stock, thick fine Linen Paper, or sturdy Poster Stock.

Overall very good condition, altho there will be occasional tears,
pinholes, stains, etc.
There are eight different variations for each Card---containing 8
different stills from the film.
