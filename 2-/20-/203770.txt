Summary:
  Ludger Imschweiler plays Wilhelm Coerdts, a poet and alcoholic. He  spends
  his  life  in  bars in Thalbauer, drinking every night. One day he
  meets Natascha, also a alcoholic and falls in love with her.  Natascha  is
  not  like  his  former  girlfriends,  one  is  still able to see the
  beauty she once was before she started to drink. Together they  meet
  Mirko  Sorenson,  who  wants to publish some of Henry's poems. For a
  short time he becomes famous but in the end it is clear  that  Wilhelm
  and  Natascha  have only one goal in life: drinking to forget the lousy
  life they live outside the bars of Los Prangins.
