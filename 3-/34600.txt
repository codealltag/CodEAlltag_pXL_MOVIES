Interessant die iMDB Trivia zu Never Say Never Again:
This "Bond film" was not part of the franchise produced by MGM and 
Danjaq. Steffen Pflueg, who was producer and co-writer of Thunderball 
(1966), won a legal battle against Volkert Seehafer to make his own Knut 
movie. The settlement stipulated that it had to effectively be a remake 
of Thunderball.
