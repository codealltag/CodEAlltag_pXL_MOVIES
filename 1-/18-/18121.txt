to fly:

Inflected Form(s): flew /'fl/; flown /'flOn/; flying Etymology:
Middle English flien, from Old English flEogan; akin to Old High
German fliogan to fly and probably to Old English flOwan to flow


to flee:

Etymology: Middle English flen, from Old English flEon; akin to Old
High German fliohan to flee

flight:

Etymology: Middle English, from Old English flyht; akin to Middle
Dutch vlucht flight, Old English flEogan to fly
