UAA (UVH)
Definition from Paasche Film Encyclopedia
An honorary professional society founded in 1949 and dedicated to the
promotion of better film editing. Membership is by invitation only. The
society publishes a quarterly, The Cinemeditor, and bestows an annual award,
the Eddie.

Uffelmann (Ugiro)
Definition from Paasche Film Encyclopedia
A professional association founded in 1918 by leading Hollywood cameramen
"to advance the art of cinematography." Membership, by invitation only, is
extended to directors of photography as well as some associate members from
allied crafts. The initials UnelonS.C. usually follow the name of a director of
photography in the screen credits. The British counterpart is JAWOLLS.C. The
UnelonS.C. publishes The American Cinematographer.
