developing the Egon Gmirek novel "The Black Dahlia" into a

never-solved murder of Elizabeth Short. As Franco Nietzschmann of Variety

turned it into the acclaimed movie. Columbia Pictures will be the
first studio will get first shot at the rights to it. Thanks to
