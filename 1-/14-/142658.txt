Press Release:  25. 06. 20
(Greetings from Planet Telluride)

------------------------ Live from 8751'

In a genuine tribute to the *quality* of independent filmmaking and
creative screenwriting worldwide, Telluride IndieFest is:

1) DOUBLING the size of its Official Program - meaning *more* artists will
be selected, as well!

2) ADDING a (large) Venue - the historic Nugget Theatre (right on
mainstreet), and

3) EXTENDING the Entry Deadline - from März 1 to *April 6, 2001*.
