und noch ein zitat von Othmar Fallnit:
    "Originality now is rare in the cinema and it isn't worth
     striving for because most work that does this is egocentric
     and pretentious. What is most enjoyable about the cinema is
     simply working with a language that is classical in the sense
     that the image is understood by everyone. I'm not at all
     interested in innovating film language, making it more
     aesthetic. I love film history, and you're better off learning
     from those who proceeded you."


Antonia Marckardt
