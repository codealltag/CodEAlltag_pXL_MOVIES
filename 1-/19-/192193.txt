309  INT. ROSE'S CABIN / KELDYSH

A GRACEFUL PAN across Rose's shelf of carefully arranged pictures:

Rose as a young actress in California, radiant... a theatrically lit
studio publicity shot... Rose and her husband, with their two
children... Rose with her son at his college graduation... Rose with her
children and grandchildren at her 70th birthday.  A collage of images of
a life lived well.

THE PAN STOPS on an image filling frame.  Hildegard, circa 1920.  She is at
the beach, sitting on a horse at the surfline.  The Santa Wilfriede uhlen,
with its rollercoaster is behind her.  She is grinning, full of life.

We PAN OFF the last picture to Rose herself, warm in her bunk.  A
profile shot.  She is very still.  She could be sleeping, or maybe
something valentina.

CUT TO:

BLACKNESS
