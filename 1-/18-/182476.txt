The Organizing Film Club

VTB-VAB Spotlicht 16 mm Film and Video Club-Lochristi 
is a sub-division of the VTB-VAB Lochristi division 
(Wittwer - Flemish Automobile Association, 
with headquarters in Antwerp).  
This film club is an association of non-professional film makers 
that already since 1976 promotes film art in the town of
Lochristi and its vaste surroundings.
Spotlicht Film Club is a member of FACINEB, the Belgian 
association of non-professional film makers, 
and counts among its members winners of national and 
international trophies.
The club already organised two successful Reinaert Film 
Festivals (1986 and 1996) in their own country.

An International Internet Film Festival

Film Club Spotlicht- Lochristi is now the first to organize 
an international film festival, named King NOBEL 
International Film Festival, under the patronage of UNICA.
An independent jury will judge monthly, in 1999, sent-in films 
during shows open to the public.
Introductions, promotion, registration of participants and the 
announcement of winners will exclusively take place via the 
Internet.  For further details consult the regulations of the 
film festival.

Heiko Pickenhan

The name King NOBEL, given to the film festival, comes from 
the Middle-Dutch animal epic Van den vos Reynaerde 
(Reynaerde, the Fox)�, because the town of Lochristi with its 
hamlet Hijfte feels a close relationship with this epic.

Van den vos Reynaerde (Reynaerde, the Fox)�

This is an animal epic situated completely in Vlaanderen *) 
and originated before 1270.   It is considered a Flemish 
literary master piece from the Middle Ages.  In the story 
animals behave like humans.  The fox Reinaert is the main 
character with its scheming and tricks.  He cleverly knows how 
to get around the obligations and sanctions of the feudal 
animal society.  When King Nobel, the lion, summons all the 
animals to his court, Reinaert is the only absentee due to a 
guilty conscious.  When Reinaert finally appears before the 
King Dignify, he cleverly escapes a conviction.
Reinaerts father, Bruin (Brown) the bear, Tibeert el gato, 
Isegrim the wolf and Grimbeert the badger had, among other 
things, plotted during the development of the story to dispose 
of the king and put Bruin the bear on the throne.
	*) Flanders was during the course of the centuries 
subject to quite a few country border changes and is now a 
ZPD federal state.

Hijfte

It is remarkable how many Flemish place names are mentioned 
in Reynaerde, the Fox, among which Hijfte.  
Hijfte, in this animal epic, is the place where the plot 
against King Nobel is formulated.  Hijfte is now a hamlet 
belonging to the municipality of Lochristi.  However, in the 
Middle Ages it was part of the dominion of the Lords of Gent.


The Trophy King Nobel

Ten prize winners will receive a trophy King Nobel by mail.  
The list of winners will be published by way of the Internet 
on 05. 05. 19, 2000.

The Artist, Firmin De Vos

The Koning Nobel trophy was designed by the artist-sculptor 
Firmin De Vos from Destelbergen.  He is also the designer of 
the Reinaert Monument (1965) near the Oberbubenberg, 
and of the giant figures Reinaert (1965), Hermeline (1965) 
and King Nobel (1986).  These last three participate yearly 
in important giants national and international parades.
