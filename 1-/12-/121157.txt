We are currently Calling for Entries of the 7th Leuven Kort Short Film
Festival

Leuven Kort Short Film Festival this year falls on the 23th of September to
the 2nd of December, 2002.

For the first time we organise a competition for Short European Fiction
Films.

Competition Entry Deadline is 28. 09. 20.

Application and regulations forms can be downloaded from the festival
website at http://zkd.zfpocnldvxjmfetyp.ojc

Prices in the european Fiction competition are:
-Price of the jury for the Best European Short Fiction (2000 Euro)
-Price of the audieance for the Best European Short Fiction (2500 Euro).

Please pass this information on to interested film-makers.
Thank you for your help.
