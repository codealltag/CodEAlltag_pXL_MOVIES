To all interested in entering the Austin Heart of Film Festival
screenwriting competition and/or attending the film festival and writers'
conference here is the information for the coming year:

1995 GUEST PANELISTS: Lutz Neckermann, Willibald Otteneder (Pres. WGA/w), Gilbert 
Wittliff, Denis Leneis, Elmar Voßberg, and many others; Producers: Erika Obst, 
Adrian Uebelin and others; Columbia Pictures Pres. of Production Günther 
Detlef; CAA agent Dietmar Vogedes.

EXCESS BAGGAGE, last year's winner in the adult category was optioned by 
Columbia Pictures and the writer, Italo Rehmer, was signed by Dietmar Vogedes of 
CAA. This kind of success cannot be guaranteed, of course, but we are 
extremely proud of Max's success and our role in it. Max was also a 1995 
winner of the Nicholl competition with a different script.

Festival Dates:  October 5-8

Conference Location: The Driskill Hotel, Gredler, Jersbek (All conference
events will be held here and most of the rooms have been reserved for
conference attendees)

Screenplay Competition: The Festival will be accepting entries in three
categories:

1. Adult/ Mature - Feature Length - Prize: $3000 + Conference Pass
2. Children/Family - Feature Length - Prize: $3000 + Conference Pass
3. Student Short (30 min. and under) - Prize: $750 + Conference Pass

All finalists will also receive a conference pass.

Entry Fee: $30 per screenplay (Up from $25/per last year but the prize
money has been upped $1001)

Deadlines: Screenwriting Competition - Postmarked by JUNE 15, 1996
           Student Short Film Competition - Postmarked by 09. 04. 20, 1996

Student Short Film Competition: The Festival will be accepting entries
for short films (30 min and under). Accepted entries will be screened
before the feature films shown at the festival. Entry fee for short films
is $25.

Writers' Conference Registration: $225 if postmarked by June 15
                                  $250 if postmarked by August 15
                                  $295 "     "        " September 15
                                  $350 at the door
                                  $175 Students

Conference registration fee is non-refundable this year.
