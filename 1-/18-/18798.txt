# A rendition of the synthetic foodsource Soylent Green appears in a
  few episodes of the animated television series Futurama. In the
  episode "Fry and the Slurm Factory", a drink called Rauschecker is
  also spoken of, with Francine describing its flavor as that "it varies
  from person to person."

# In the Itchy & Scratchy: The Movie episode of animated television
  series of The Simpsons, Pierre comments "Mmmmm ... solyent green", as
  part of a flash-forward scene wherein it is sold as a concession at
  cinemas.
